Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

At Cardhawk, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, state/location, and password
  • Card Preferences: Your selected credit cards and customized point valuations
  • Spending Data: Monthly spending amounts you enter in our calculator (optional)
  • Feedback & Support: Messages and feedback you submit through our app

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and time spent in the app
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP address, timestamps, and error reports

1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

Important: We NEVER collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your actual credit card numbers
  • CVV codes or security codes
  • Card expiration dates
  • Transaction history or banking data
  • Social Security Numbers or financial account details

Cardhawk only stores references to publicly available credit card products (e.g., "Chase Sapphire Preferred"). We never handle your actual card credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our credit card recommendation service
  • Personalize your experience with custom card selections and preferences
  • Send important account notifications and security alerts
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our app
  • Send optional marketing emails (only if you opt-in)
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted service providers who help us operate Cardhawk:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services (data encrypted at rest)
  • Vercel: Web hosting and content delivery
  • Analytics Providers: To understand how users interact with our app (anonymized data)

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing using bcrypt
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to user data
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing monitoring and vulnerability assessments

However, no system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights & Choices

5.1 Access & Export

You can view and export your data at any time through your account settings.

5.2 Correction & Update

You can update your account information in Settings > Edit Profile.

5.3 Deletion

You can request account deletion, which will permanently remove your personal data within 30 days. Email us at privacy@cardhawk.app.

5.4 Email Communications

You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in our emails or by updating your email preferences.

5.5 Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences (theme, card selections)
  • Analyze app usage (analytics cookies)

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect app functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Cardhawk is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

Cardhawk is based in the United States. If you access our service from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and stored in the US. By using Cardhawk, you consent to this transfer.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

9. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to credit card issuers and third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies.

10. Affiliate Relationships

We may earn commissions from credit card issuers when you apply for cards through our referral links. This does not affect our recommendations or your privacy. We do not share your personal information with issuers unless you choose to apply for their cards.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of Cardhawk after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

  • Email: privacy@cardhawk.app
  • In-App: Settings > Send Feedback
  • Response Time: We respond within 48 hours

13. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics
  • Legal/compliance data retained as required by law

Your privacy matters to us. Thank you for trusting Cardhawk with your credit card optimization journey.
